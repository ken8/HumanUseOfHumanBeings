How to use this course

A quick guide to all the features available to you.

Course structure

This course is organized into modules, each covering a key section of the source material. Every module includes an overview and summary, key ideas to retain, two worked examples, a hands-on exercise, a five-question quiz with explained answers, and a teach-back prompt. Work them in order the first time; jump freely after that.

Progress tracking

Tap "Mark this module complete" at the bottom of each module when you can pass the quiz and handle the teach-back prompt. The sidebar shows a checkmark for completed modules and the progress bar at the top tracks your overall completion. Progress saves automatically in your browser.

Quizzes

Each quiz has five questions. Tap any answer to lock it in — the correct answer highlights in green and an explanation appears immediately. Your score shows once all five questions are answered. Every explanation tells you why the right answer is right, not just that it is.

Themes

Open Settings (the gear icon in the top bar) and pick from five visual themes: Control Room (the default), Blueprint, Parchment, Terminal, and Midnight. Your choice is saved automatically.

Text-to-Speech (Listen mode)

Each section inside a module has a small Listen button next to its label. Tap it to hear the section read aloud using your browser's speech synthesis. A floating control bar appears with play/pause, stop, and rewind buttons. You can choose which voice and speed to use in Settings.

In Settings, you can also enable "Highlight spoken word" to visually track which word is being read, and "Show reading progress bar" to see a progress indicator under each section label while it's being read aloud.

Note: Voices available depend on your operating system and browser. Chrome on Windows/Mac has the widest selection. Mobile browsers may have limited voices. Word highlighting accuracy varies by browser.

Background music

In Settings, you can load background music two ways: paste a YouTube URL and tap Load URL, or click "Choose MP3 or MP4 file…" to pick an audio or video file from your computer. A small player widget appears at the bottom-right corner with play/pause and volume controls. Great for lo-fi study beats or ambient soundscapes while you read. Only one source plays at a time — loading a new one stops the previous.

Note: Local files play directly in your browser and are not uploaded anywhere. YouTube URLs are saved between sessions; local files need to be re-selected each visit.

Keyboard shortcuts

Esc — close any open panel (settings, help, chatbot, certificate, or mobile menu).

Ask about the course (Chatbot)

Tap the chat bubble icon in the top bar to open the Q&A panel. Type any question about the course material and the chatbot will search through all module content to find the most relevant answer, including which module it came from.

Certificate

Tap the medal icon in the top bar to see your certificate status. Complete all module quizzes with a passing average score (default 70%) to unlock your certificate. Enter your full name and tap "Generate PDF" to download a professionally formatted completion certificate with the date.

Resetting

To clear all progress, use the "Reset progress" link at the bottom of the sidebar. Theme, voice, and music settings are saved separately and persist even after a reset.
